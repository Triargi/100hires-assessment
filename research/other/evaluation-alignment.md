## 5. Not Scored By Volume

The project prioritizes high-signal sources over raw volume.

Examples:

- YouTube transcript counts vary by expert because only relevant public videos with usable transcripts were kept.
- Current examples: Rob Illidge has 3 YouTube transcript files, Wensy Antoli has 1, and Alex Boyd, MJ Smith, Anthony Pierri, and Eva Johanna Egg each have 4.
- Wensy Antoli's personal LinkedIn posts were limited publicly, so related LinkedIn Creative Labs materials were labeled clearly instead of pretending they were personal posts.
- LinkedIn posts are summarized with short excerpts and source notes rather than copied in full.

## 6. Research Judgment

- LinkedIn access restrictions.

- Source confidence levels.
- Transcript quality limitations.
