## Short Excerpt

"Employee advocacy is when employees share company content, industry insights, or their own professional expertise."

## Research Summary

Rob defines employee advocacy as a B2B growth motion where employees share expertise and company perspective through their own professional presence. The strategic idea is that people are often more trusted than brand accounts, especially in considered B2B buying journeys. Advocacy works best when employees can show expertise authentically, not when they simply repost corporate copy.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, employee advocacy can expand reach beyond the company page and make the product category easier to understand. Sales, customer success, product, and implementation teams often hold the most useful buyer insight. Turning that insight into LinkedIn content helps educate the market and creates warmer sales conversations.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use employee voices to build trust around complex SaaS categories.
- Let teams share expertise in their own voice.
- Connect advocacy themes to buyer education and sales enablement.
