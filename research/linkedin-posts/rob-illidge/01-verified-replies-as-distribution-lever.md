## Short Excerpt

"LinkedIn now gives verified users priority in post engagement."

## Research Summary

Rob discusses LinkedIn's verified comment filter and argues that verification may become more than a status badge. If users can filter comments to show verified members, then verified employees, founders, and executives may gain more visibility in high-value comment threads. The post frames comments as an important distribution layer, not just a response mechanism.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this suggests that organic LinkedIn strategy should include verified executive and employee profiles. A verified founder, sales leader, product leader, or subject-matter expert commenting on buyer, analyst, partner, or category posts can create trust and visibility without publishing a new post every time.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Treat comments as a distribution channel.
- Encourage key advocates to verify profiles where appropriate.
- Prioritize thoughtful comments in buyer-relevant and category-relevant threads.
