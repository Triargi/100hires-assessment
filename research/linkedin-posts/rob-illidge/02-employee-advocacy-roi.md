## Short Excerpt

"How do I prove it's working?"

## Research Summary

Rob focuses on the measurement problem behind employee advocacy. He argues that advocacy needs an ROI framework that goes beyond likes and impressions. Useful signals include reach, engagement, website clicks, earned media value, demo requests, job applications, pipeline influence, and qualitative proof such as prospects referencing employee posts during sales conversations.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, employee advocacy should be connected to revenue-adjacent metrics. A SaaS team can track UTM traffic from employee posts, demo page visits, profile views from target accounts, sales conversations influenced by content, and closed-won journeys where buyers viewed employee or company LinkedIn activity.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Build measurement before scaling advocacy.
- Track commercial and trust-building signals, not vanity metrics alone.
- Use ROI reporting to secure leadership support and long-term participation.
