## Research Summary

Vulse shared LinkedIn engagement data showing that carousels/document posts, video, image, link, and text formats perform differently. The update also highlights the value of replying to comments and maintaining posting consistency. The broader message is that organic LinkedIn strategy should be informed by content format, comment behavior, and cadence rather than only by generic posting volume.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this supports a mixed-format content system. Use carousels for frameworks and buyer education, video for founder or expert trust, images for proof and social context, and text posts for sharp POVs. Pair this with employee comment participation so posts become conversations instead of isolated broadcasts.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use carousels and document posts for deeper educational content.
- Reply to comments quickly and thoughtfully to extend conversations.
- Maintain consistent employee and founder posting rather than chasing isolated viral posts.
