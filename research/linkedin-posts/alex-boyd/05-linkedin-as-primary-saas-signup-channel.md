## Short Excerpt

"My LinkedIn is the #1 source of signups."

## Research Summary

Alex shares what was working for Aware after 2.5 years of building: saying true and insightful things loudly, building in public, showing humanity, and partnering with other founders. He reports that LinkedIn was the top source of signups for Aware, ahead of SEO at the time, and says this came after years of preparing the ground.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this is a strong case study for founder-led organic LinkedIn. The channel can generate signups when the founder consistently builds trust, shows the product journey, and connects with the right market over a long period. The important lesson is that LinkedIn works as a compounding trust channel, not a short-term posting hack.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use strong, specific POVs to attract the right audience.
- Build in public to create trust and product familiarity.
- Treat founder relationships and audience-building as part of acquisition.
