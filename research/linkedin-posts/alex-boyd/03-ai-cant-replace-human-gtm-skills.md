## Short Excerpt

"AI can't join an enterprise committee buying call."

## Research Summary

Alex pushes back on AI hype by listing GTM and business skills that still require human judgment: content strategy, lead generation, networking calls, customer empathy, community building, social media strategy, strategic narrative development, and reading enterprise buying politics. His point is not that AI is useless, but that it cannot replace the high-context work that makes GTM effective.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this is a warning against automating LinkedIn content into sameness. AI can help draft, summarize, or repurpose, but the best organic strategy still depends on founder judgment, customer insight, narrative clarity, and firsthand market experience. Buyers respond to lived expertise more than generic AI-polished content.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use AI as support, not as the source of the point of view.
- Build content from customer calls, sales context, and founder judgment.
- Preserve human specificity in LinkedIn posts, especially for enterprise SaaS.
