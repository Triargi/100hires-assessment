## Research Summary

Richard responds to criticism of his Algorithm Report and emphasizes the importance of research methodology. He contrasts large longitudinal datasets with small samples and anecdotal claims. For organic LinkedIn strategy, the useful point is that teams should not build strategy from isolated screenshots, single-profile anecdotes, or fashionable tactics without understanding the underlying data quality.

## B2B SaaS Application

B2B SaaS teams often overreact to short-term platform chatter: one viral post, one influencer's tactic, or one sudden drop in impressions. This post supports a more disciplined approach: evaluate LinkedIn advice by sample size, recency, profile type, market context, and whether the metric maps to pipeline or trust.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Vet LinkedIn tactics before operationalizing them.
- Prioritize evidence from larger datasets and repeatable patterns.
- Separate algorithm folklore from measurable buyer engagement.
