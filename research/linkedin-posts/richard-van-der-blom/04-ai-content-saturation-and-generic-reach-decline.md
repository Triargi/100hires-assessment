## Short Excerpt

"Organic reach will drop another 25% for generic content."

## Research Summary

Richard warns that LinkedIn in 2026 will become more competitive as AI-generated content increases. He predicts more AI-assisted posts, lower reach for generic content, greater value from comments by verified humans, and higher pressure on visual hooks. The broader message is that volume alone will not create durable organic performance.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this is a warning against AI-powered content volume without strategic differentiation. Generic educational posts, recycled listicles, and bland category commentary will be easier to produce and easier to ignore. SaaS brands should use AI to support research and drafting, while preserving expert voice, proprietary data, customer insight, and strong opinion.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use AI to sharpen distinctive thinking, not to flood the feed.
- Prioritize original examples, data, and field insight.
- Build human engagement through thoughtful comments and expert replies.
