## Research Summary

Richard outlines five systems for building a B2B LinkedIn strategy: authority, comment-first engagement, DM conversion, profile optimization, and a broader operating cadence. The post emphasizes proof over vague thought leadership, strategic comments over passive posting, and relationship-led DMs rather than pitch-heavy automation.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this can be turned into a practical organic growth operating model. Publish proof of customer outcomes, comment on ideal customer posts, turn engagement into thoughtful conversations, and make founder or executive profiles work like landing pages. This is especially useful for early-stage SaaS teams that need trust and demand before paid channels are efficient.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Build a repeatable system, not a random posting habit.
- Use comments as a distribution and relationship channel.
- Connect content, profile, DMs, and proof into one revenue motion.
