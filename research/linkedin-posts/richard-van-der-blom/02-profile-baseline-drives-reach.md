## Short Excerpt

"Half your LinkedIn reach is decided before you hit post."

## Research Summary

Richard summarizes a finding from his 2026 Algorithm Report: a large share of LinkedIn reach is influenced before publishing by profile baseline factors such as followers, history, past engagement, and topic consistency. He contrasts this with post-level reactions and comments, which many creators over-focus on. The deeper message is that reach compounds through profile identity and consistent audience signals over time.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this makes profile strategy part of content strategy. Founder and executive profiles should clearly communicate the category, buyer problem, credibility, and point of view. If profiles say one thing while posts cover scattered topics, the algorithm and audience receive mixed signals.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Optimize expert profiles as part of the organic content system.
- Keep headline, About section, Featured content, and recurring posts aligned.
- Build reach through months of consistent relevance, not single-post tricks.
