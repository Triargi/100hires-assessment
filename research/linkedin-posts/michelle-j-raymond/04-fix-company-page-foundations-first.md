## Research Summary

Michelle breaks down common Company Page problems: neglected pages, pages that behave like internal newsletters, and pages that have drifted away from strategy. The core idea is that before posting more, B2B teams need to fix the basics: clear About section, complete details, relevant banner, audience-focused content, and a reason for buyers to stay.

She warns that a Company Page full of internal announcements may serve the business but not the audience.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, the Company Page should quickly answer who the product helps, what problem it solves, what makes it credible, and why the visitor should care. SaaS buyers often check the Page during vendor research, so a neglected or internally focused Page can create doubt at the worst possible moment.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Audit the Page before scaling content output.
- Protect the Page from becoming a dumping ground for internal requests.
- Make the Page useful to buyers, not only informative for employees.
