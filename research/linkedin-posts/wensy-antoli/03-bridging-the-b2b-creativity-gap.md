## Research Summary

This event page positions B2B marketing as moving beyond transactional, one-size-fits-all tactics toward more creative, human, and personalized experiences. Wensy is listed as a LinkedIn Creative Strategy Lead on a panel about B2B creativity. The page emphasizes memorability, storytelling, emotional connection, thought leadership, interactive demos, and video storytelling.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, the lesson is that safe content often becomes invisible content. Organic LinkedIn should help buyers remember the problem, the brand, and the POV. SaaS teams can do this through distinctive category narratives, recognizable content formats, human voices, and buyer-specific examples.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Prioritize memorability, not only information density.
- Make technical SaaS content more human and specific.
- Use creative formats to make category POVs easier to recall.
