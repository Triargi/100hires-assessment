## Research Summary

This webinar page argues that personality and emotional marketing are underused in B2B, despite evidence that emotive marketing can improve engagement for tech brands. Wensy is featured as a LinkedIn creative strategy lead helping brands unlock the creative potential of the platform. The central idea is that B2B content does not need to be sterile to be credible.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, emotional content can make functional products easier to connect with. Instead of only posting feature lists, SaaS teams can show buyer frustration, user relief, team conviction, customer confidence, and the cost of staying with old workflows. This can strengthen organic LinkedIn content by making it more memorable and more shareable.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Add personality without sacrificing clarity.
- Show the emotional stakes behind functional SaaS problems.
- Use credible human storytelling to differentiate in crowded categories.
