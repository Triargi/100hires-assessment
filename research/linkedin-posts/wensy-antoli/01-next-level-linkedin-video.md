## Research Summary

This Sprout Social webinar page frames LinkedIn video as a major B2B engagement opportunity, citing 154 billion annual video views and rising video engagement on LinkedIn. Wensy is positioned as a LinkedIn expert on visual content and emotional impact. The session focuses on short-form video, emotional storytelling, and content that builds brand recognition and lasting connections.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this supports using video beyond product demos. Founder clips, customer problem explainers, behind-the-scenes product workflow videos, and SME-led short videos can make technical products feel more human and memorable. The goal is not just reach; it is helping buyers remember the product category and trust the people behind it.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Treat video as a trust and memory asset, not only a format.
- Use short-form clips to explain complex SaaS workflows simply.
- Pair product clarity with emotional storytelling.
