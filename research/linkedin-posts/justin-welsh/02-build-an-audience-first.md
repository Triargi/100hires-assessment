## Short Excerpt

"Try building an audience first."

## Research Summary

Justin presents audience building as the starting point for creating more options. The post lists low-cost ways to monetize expertise, then points back to building an audience as the practical first move. For organic LinkedIn strategy, the implied order is: build trust and attention first, then convert that attention into consulting, products, offers, or inbound opportunities.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS teams, this translates to building an audience before pushing demo requests. A company can use LinkedIn to educate a specific buying committee, document category problems, and build familiarity over time. This is especially useful for early-stage SaaS companies that need trust before prospects are ready to book a demo.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Grow a relevant audience before asking for pipeline.
- Use educational content to create future demand.
- Measure trust signals and qualified conversations, not only impressions.
