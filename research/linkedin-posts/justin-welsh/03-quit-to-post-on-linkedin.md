## Research Summary

Justin connects his prior executive experience to the decision to publish on LinkedIn. The post is less tactical than some of his other content, but it is useful because it shows how career credibility can become distribution. The message is that expertise becomes more valuable when it is made public and shaped into consistent content.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this is a case for using executive experience as content capital. CROs, founders, product leaders, and customer success leaders can turn firsthand operating lessons into high-trust posts. Buyers often want evidence that a company understands their world; executive-led content can create that evidence before a sales call.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Turn internal expertise into public proof of competence.
- Use founder and executive stories to create trust and context.
- Make the company's category experience visible through personal profiles.
