## Short Excerpt

"Attention comes before the offer."

## Research Summary

Justin argues that creators and entrepreneurs often build products in silence, then wonder why no one buys. His tactical advice is to publish opinions about what frustrates you in your industry, post consistently for a set period, reply to people in your niche, and DM people who engage without immediately pitching. The post is directly relevant to organic LinkedIn strategy because it treats attention as a prerequisite for launch.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this maps cleanly to pre-launch and category-education content. Before launching a feature, report, or product narrative, a SaaS team can publish strong points of view about broken workflows, outdated best practices, and costly buyer misconceptions. This creates a warm audience before the commercial ask.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Build attention before launching offers or campaigns.
- Turn industry irritations into opinion-led posts.
- Pair posting with daily commenting and light-touch DMs.
