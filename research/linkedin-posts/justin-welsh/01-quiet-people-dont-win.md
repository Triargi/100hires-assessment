## Short Excerpt

"Quiet people don't win."

## Research Summary

Justin argues that strong work is not enough if the market never sees it. The post frames visibility as an active behavior: posting ideas, sending DMs, joining conversations, and becoming known for a point of view. The core lesson is that opportunity often flows to people who make their expertise visible, not only to the most technically qualified people.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this supports founder-led and expert-led LinkedIn strategy. A founder, product leader, or revenue leader should consistently publish practical observations about the category, buyer pain, market shifts, and customer problems. The goal is to make the company discoverable before buyers enter a formal vendor evaluation.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Treat visibility as distribution infrastructure.
- Publish the point of view behind the product, not just product updates.
- Use comments and DMs to turn attention into real conversations.
