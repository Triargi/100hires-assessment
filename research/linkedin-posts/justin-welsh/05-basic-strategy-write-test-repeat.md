## Short Excerpt

"Write stuff online. See what worked."

## Research Summary

Justin describes his early strategy as simple experimentation: publish, observe what works, repeat what works, and reduce what does not. He contrasts this with overbuilt strategies, complex funnels, and detailed plans that never turn into publishing behavior. The post is useful because it reduces organic LinkedIn strategy to a repeatable learning loop.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS teams, this is a practical operating model for LinkedIn. Instead of waiting for a perfect content calendar, publish consistent posts around ICP pain, product beliefs, objections, customer lessons, category narratives, and founder POV. Then review which posts generate profile views, saves, comments from target buyers, DMs, and demo intent.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use LinkedIn as a market feedback loop.
- Prefer consistent publishing plus review over abstract planning.
- Double down on themes that attract qualified buyer attention.
