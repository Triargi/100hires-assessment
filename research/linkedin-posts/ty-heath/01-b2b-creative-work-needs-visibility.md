## Research Summary

Ty announces the 2026 Creative B2B Lions jury and frames the group as a global cross-section of people shaping B2B creative work. The post is not a tactical content playbook, but it signals an important organic strategy principle: B2B brands grow when they treat creativity as a serious business asset and make their work visible to the market.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, the lesson is to stop hiding creative strategy inside campaigns, sales decks, and internal launches. Turn strong product narratives, customer stories, category POVs, and market insights into public LinkedIn content. This helps SaaS teams build mental availability before buyers are ready to evaluate vendors.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use LinkedIn to make B2B creative thinking visible.
- Promote category POVs, not only feature announcements.
- Treat creative credibility as part of demand creation.
