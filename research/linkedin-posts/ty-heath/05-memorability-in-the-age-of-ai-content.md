## Short Excerpt

"Be memorable."

## Research Summary

Ty argues that AI has made content production faster and cheaper, but that sameness raises the value of distinctiveness. She emphasizes that buyers remember brands, trust discerning voices, and return to experiences that create connection. The post reframes the AI content question away from speed and toward meaning, memory, and differentiation.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this is a warning against using AI to flood LinkedIn with generic thought leadership. Organic content should sharpen the brand's distinctive point of view, voice, examples, and proof. The strategic advantage is not producing more posts; it is becoming more recognizable and easier to recall when a buyer has a problem.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use AI to support clarity, not to create generic volume.
- Prioritize distinctiveness and memory over posting frequency alone.
- Build recognizable brand codes across founder, executive, and company content.
