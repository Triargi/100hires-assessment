## Short Excerpt

"Many ideas stall in translation."

## Research Summary

Ty shares the CTO Test: Compression, Transmission, and Ownership. The idea is that a strategy must be simple enough to compress, repeatable enough to transmit, and participatory enough for others to own. If an idea only works when the original author is in the room, it is not ready to scale.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS LinkedIn content, this is a strong test for category narratives and thought leadership. The best organic posts should make the company's belief easy to repeat. A SaaS positioning idea should fit into a memorable sentence, survive reposting or internal sharing, and give customers, partners, and employees language they want to use.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Compress complex SaaS ideas into repeatable language.
- Test whether prospects can explain your POV without your team present.
- Design LinkedIn posts to become shareable internal ammunition for champions.
