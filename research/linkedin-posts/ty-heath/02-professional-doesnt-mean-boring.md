## Short Excerpt

"Professional doesn't mean boring."

## Research Summary

Ty argues that "professional" has too often become code for safe, polished, and easy to ignore. She points out that B2B buyers spend time on social platforms, discover tools there, learn from creators, and validate decisions in communities. The strategic question is whether enterprise brands understand the culture of the feed.

## B2B SaaS Application

This is directly useful for B2B SaaS organic LinkedIn strategy. SaaS brands should publish content that feels native to LinkedIn instead of repurposing sterile product copy. Founder POVs, executive voice, creator collaborations, customer-led stories, and category commentary can all make the brand more human and memorable.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Make content culturally fluent, not just formally polished.
- Elevate founder and executive voices.
- Use platform-native storytelling instead of brochure-style posts.
