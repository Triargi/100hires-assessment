## Short Excerpt

"Your champion believes in you."

## Research Summary

Ty describes a common B2B buying problem: a single champion may understand the product, but the rest of the buying group may not know the vendor well enough to feel safe. She cites research indicating that won deals are much more likely when awareness is broad across the buying group early in the process.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this supports a LinkedIn strategy that targets the full buying committee, not only the obvious user persona. Organic content should speak to finance, legal, IT, executives, end users, and internal champions in different ways. The aim is to make the SaaS brand familiar across the room before the champion has to defend the decision.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Create content for the whole buying committee.
- Build familiarity before sales conversations peak.
- Help champions retell the value in language their stakeholders trust.
