## Short Excerpt

"Most LinkedIn agencies start with ghostwriting."

## Research Summary

Eva describes how LinkedIn agencies often start with ghostwriting, then expand into positioning, strategy, outreach, reporting, and broader revenue services when results become inconsistent. Her argument is that agencies need content execution connected to growth: fast, scalable, data-informed, and built on what is working now rather than one-off posts.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this maps to the gap between "posting" and pipeline. A SaaS team should connect founder posts, company content, repurposed assets, performance data, and buyer intent into one execution system. Otherwise, content becomes a set of disconnected posts with inconsistent commercial impact.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Do not judge LinkedIn strategy by likes alone.
- Connect content production to pipeline signals and audience learning.
- Repurpose podcasts, videos, docs, and internal expertise into LinkedIn content quickly.
