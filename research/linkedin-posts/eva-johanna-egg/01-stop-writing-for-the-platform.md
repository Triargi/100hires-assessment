## Short Excerpt

"You started writing for it. Instead of just saying what you think."

## Research Summary

Eva argues that many people start disliking LinkedIn because they over-optimize for the platform. They ask what will perform, what the audience wants, and how to phrase something "on brand," until the content could have been written by anyone. Her counter-position is that the hard-to-copy advantage in the AI era is a person who knows what they think and says it clearly.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS founders and marketers, this is a reminder that organic LinkedIn should not become algorithm cosplay. The strongest content comes from a clear point of view about the market, product, buyer pain, and category. AI and templates can help production, but the underlying opinion must still feel owned.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Optimize for clarity of thought before algorithm performance.
- Build founder and SME content around real opinions, not generic best practices.
- Use AI to support expression, not erase personality.
