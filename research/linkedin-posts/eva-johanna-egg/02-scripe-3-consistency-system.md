## Research Summary

Eva frames Scripe 3.0 as a rebuild around the real barriers to LinkedIn consistency: starting, choosing what matters, and maintaining momentum. The post says the product was rebuilt after deep user conversations and analysis of thousands of posts, with the goal of making content creation feel guided rather than lonely or chaotic.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS teams, organic LinkedIn strategy needs a repeatable workflow, not only a content calendar. Founders and experts often have ideas but struggle with prioritization, cadence, and format. A useful content system should reduce decision fatigue while keeping the expert's voice and category narrative intact.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Build a content workflow that helps experts know what to say next.
- Treat consistency as a systems problem, not only a discipline problem.
- Use user conversations and performance data to improve the content process.
