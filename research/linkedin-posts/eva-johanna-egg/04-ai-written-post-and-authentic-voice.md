## Short Excerpt

"My last post hit 50,000 impressions. And it was 100% written by AI."

## Research Summary

Eva uses a high-performing AI-written post to challenge the assumption that AI-assisted content is automatically inauthentic. The implied lesson is that AI output can work when the underlying inputs, voice, and point of view are strong. The issue is not whether AI touched the draft; the issue is whether the content still represents a real person and useful idea.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, AI can help scale founder-led and expert-led LinkedIn content, but it needs strong source material: customer calls, founder beliefs, product insights, audience pain points, and examples. AI should accelerate drafting and repurposing, while the team protects voice, accuracy, and strategic relevance.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use AI to amplify a real voice, not manufacture one.
- Feed AI with specific insight and clear positioning.
- Review AI content for accuracy, originality, and buyer relevance before publishing.
