## Short Excerpt

"Building a GOOD product? A product that solves a real problem, delights users, and stands out? That's a different game."

## Research Summary

Eva pushes back against the narrative that SaaS success is easy because AI, no-code, and templates make products faster to ship. She argues that building something valuable still requires product engineering, design, marketing, community, support, feedback loops, and long-term commitment. The post positions Scripe as a product built through hard-earned work rather than hype.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS organic LinkedIn, this is a strong founder-led content pattern: challenge a popular narrative, show the reality behind the product, and build trust by being honest about the work. Buyers and partners are more likely to trust a founder who shows judgment, constraints, and commitment than one who only posts wins.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Use build-in-public content to create trust, not just attention.
- Contrast hype with the real work behind the product.
- Show the operating reality that makes the SaaS credible.
