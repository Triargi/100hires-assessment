## Short Excerpt

"You can grow your LinkedIn audience without needing to tell everyone they are getting left behind."

## Research Summary

Anthony criticizes AI panic content and the tendency to use fear-based claims such as "AI just killed X" or "you are being left behind." He argues that AI can be powerful without being treated as magical or infallible, and that hallucination is not the same as human error. The bigger content lesson is that anxiety-driven posts may produce engagement, but can damage long-term brand trust.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, especially AI SaaS, organic LinkedIn should avoid fear as the primary hook. Stronger content explains real use cases, limitations, implementation context, and buyer risk. This builds trust with sophisticated B2B buyers who are tired of hype.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Do not build a brand on panic hooks.
- Use nuance to build credibility in noisy categories.
- Be honest about product limitations and buyer risk.
