# Why AI vs Why Your AI

## Short Excerpt

"Are your prospects asking 'why should we use AI?' Or are they asking 'why should we use YOUR AI?'"

## Research Summary

Anthony distinguishes between two messaging jobs for AI companies. If the market is immature, messaging must explain why AI agents are worth considering at all. If the market is already evaluating or using agents, messaging must explain why this specific AI product is different or better than alternatives. The post warns against answering the wrong buyer question.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS organic LinkedIn, this is a reminder to match content to market awareness. Posts for immature buyers should educate on the problem, workflow, and category. Posts for mature buyers should compare alternatives, name tradeoffs, and clarify differentiation. A SaaS founder who skips this distinction risks talking past the buyer.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Identify whether the audience needs category education or vendor differentiation.
- Write LinkedIn posts that answer the buyer's current question.
- Align profile, posts, and homepage around the same level of market maturity.
