## Short Excerpt

"You now compete with literally every other AI tool on the market."

## Research Summary

Anthony argues that leading with AI in messaging creates a positioning problem. Buyers may not care about a vendor's internal distinctions between chatbot, agent platform, agentic OS, or similar labels. If the buyer sees all of them as AI tools, then the company must explain why its AI product is the right choice relative to the alternatives.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS LinkedIn content, "we use AI" is no longer a strong message by itself. SaaS companies need posts that clarify who the product is for, what workflow it improves, what it replaces, why the approach is different, and what tradeoff the buyer is making. This turns AI messaging from a broad buzzword into buyer-relevant positioning.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Avoid generic "AI-powered" positioning.
- Name the competitive alternative in the buyer's mind.
- Use LinkedIn content to make differentiation concrete and memorable.
