## Short Excerpt

"A list of firmographics doesn't constitute a marketable segment."

## Research Summary

Anthony argues that many companies define target customers as a collection of firmographics, such as size, industry, region, or employee count. That does not guarantee the companies actually need or want the product. A stronger segment starts from the buyer's relationship to a problem, workflow, product category, or existing alternative.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS LinkedIn strategy, this means content should be written for a segment with shared needs, not a demographic spreadsheet. Instead of posting for "enterprise SaaS companies with 1,000 employees," write for teams actively evaluating a replacement, stuck in a specific workflow, or dissatisfied with a current workaround.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Define content audiences by need and context, not just firmographics.
- Write posts for a recognizable buyer situation.
- Use segmentation clarity to make hooks, examples, and CTAs more specific.
