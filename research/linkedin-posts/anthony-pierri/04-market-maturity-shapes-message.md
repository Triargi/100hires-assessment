## Research Summary

Anthony shares a market maturity framework developed from hundreds of positioning projects. The idea is that buyers in immature, emerging, and mature markets need different messages. Immature buyers may need the problem and category explained; emerging buyers may need help choosing an approach; mature buyers may already understand the category and need clear differentiation.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS organic LinkedIn, content pillars should reflect market maturity. A new category needs posts that create problem awareness and name the stakes. A crowded category needs comparison, tradeoff, and differentiation content. A team that misreads maturity will publish posts that feel polished but fail to move buyers.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Diagnose market maturity before building a content calendar.
- Use education, comparison, or differentiation depending on buyer awareness.
- Make LinkedIn posts match the mental stage of the market.
