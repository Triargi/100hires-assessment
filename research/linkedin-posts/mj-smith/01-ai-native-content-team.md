## Research Summary

MJ describes hiring an Editor in Chief to build an AI-native content team at CoLab. The key idea is that AI should not create random one-off content. Instead, the team will build content series with human-defined strategic guardrails, then use AI agents to help scan data sources, identify topics and hooks, prepare SME interviews, turn insights into content, and repurpose across channels.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS, this is a strong model for using AI without sacrificing expertise. SaaS teams can design repeatable content series around buyer pains, customer proof, product workflows, technical objections, or industry shifts. AI can support production, but humans should define the narrative, audience, and quality bar.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Build content series, not isolated posts.
- Use AI for workflow leverage, not generic thought leadership.
- Ground content in SME interviews, internal data, and external market signals.
