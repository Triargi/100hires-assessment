## Short Excerpt

"Your first move as a new marketing leader shouldn't be brand refresh + new website."

## Research Summary

MJ argues that new marketing leaders should be careful about jumping straight into a brand refresh or website rebuild. The strategic point is about sequencing: before changing visible brand assets, marketers need to test and refine the narrative in places closer to the customer, such as sales conversations, sales decks, and internal alignment. Her related strategic narrative article explains how CoLab built a narrative around showing winners and losers in the customer's world.

## B2B SaaS Application

For B2B SaaS organic LinkedIn, this means content strategy should not start with surface-level branding. Start by clarifying the strategic narrative: what changed in the buyer's world, why old approaches fail, what new approach wins, and why the SaaS product matters now. LinkedIn posts can then test these messages before they become website copy, campaigns, or sales decks.

## Content Strategy Takeaway

- Test narrative before committing to a full brand refresh.
- Use LinkedIn to validate language, pain points, and category framing.
- Build content from strategic narrative, not isolated promotional ideas.
